## FAQ

**Q: Do I have to fill out a long form?**
A: No! Just click the icon that matches your problem.

**Q: Where do I find my ticket code?**
A: In the confirmation email and at the top of the ticket details.

**Q: What if I picked the wrong fault type?**
A: Support staff can update it, or you can create a new ticket.

**Q: How do I know if my ticket is being worked on?**
A: Status will show "In Progress" and you can see the timer.

**Q: What if my issue is really urgent?**
A: Select "Urgent" priority for 2-hour guaranteed resolution time.

**Q: Can I see all my tickets?**
A: Yes, click on "Tickets" in the dashboard to see your ticket history.

**Q: Who can see my tickets?**
A: You, the support team, and administrators. Your ticket is private to the organization.
